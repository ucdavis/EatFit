Tostadas

Ingredients:
2 c. red leaf lettuce
1 can nonfat refried beans
4 oz. reduced fat cheddar cheese, shredded
1 c. masa harina mix (corn tortilla mix)
3/4 c. + 2 T water
1 c. salsa

Materials:
Large bowl
Wooden spoons
Wax paper
Frying pan or griddle
Broiler pan
Measuring cup
Spatula
Cheese grater
Cutting board
Knife

Directions:
1. Wash hands thoroughly
2. Mix masa harina mix with water.
3. Stir until water is abosrbed and mixture is sticky. Add more water if needed.
4. Separate into 8 equal balls, about the size of a large golf ball.
5. Make a tortilla by flattening out each ball between wax paper or with hands.
6. Place tortilla into very hot skillet, cooking each side twice for about 30 seconds each time.
7. Remove from skillet and place on broiler pan.
8. Once all tortillas are on pan, place pan in oven on broil.
9. Flip tortillas every minute or so until crispy (about 10 minutes).
10. Layer tostada shell with beans, cheese, lettuce, and salsa. Enjoy.

Nutrition Information Per Serving (2 each):
Calories: 172
% of calories from fat: 38%
Sugar: 5 gm
Calcium: 25%
Iron: 11%
Vitamin C: 49%
Vitamin A: 18%
Servings of fruits and vegetables: 1.5
