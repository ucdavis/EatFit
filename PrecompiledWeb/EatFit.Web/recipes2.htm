Peanut Butter Energy Ball


Ingredients:
1/4 c. reduced fat peanut butter
1/4 c. honey
1/2 c. dry powdered nonfat milk
1 1/2 c. corn flakes cereal


Materials:
Medium bowl
Spatula
Measuring cups
Wooden spoon
Small bowl
Wax paper

Directions:
1. Wash hands well.
2. Mix peanut butter, honey, and powdered milk until no clumps are present.
3. Mix in 1 cup of corn flakes until evenly distributed.
4. Roll into 8 ping-pong size balls and place on wax paper.
5. Crush remainder of corn flakes in a bowl.
6. Roll balls in corn flakes to coat outside.
7. Place balls in plastic wrap or air tight containers.
8. Refrigerate if you want them chewy. Store at room temperature if you want them softer.

Serves 4

Nutrition Information Per Serving (2 balls):
Calories: 220
% of calories from fat: 26%
Sugar: 24 gm
Calcium: 16%
Iron: 4%
Vitamin C: 8%
Vitamin A: 9%
