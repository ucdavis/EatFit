Minestrone Soup


Ingredients:
2 c. mixed frozen vegetables
1 celery stalk
1 15 oz. can stewed tomatoes
2 1/4 c. chicken broth
7.5 oz. of kidney beans
1/2 c. dry macaroni noodles
1/2 large onion

Materials:
Large pot
Cutting board
Measuring cups
Can opener
Knife


Directions:
1) Wash hands thoroughly.
2) Drain beans and rinse until water is clear.
3) Chop celery stalk and onion into small pieces.
4) Add all ingredients into pot.
5) Bring to a boil.
6) Cover pot and reduce heat.
7) Simmer for 6 to 8 minutes until pasta is cooked.
8) Spoon into bowls and enjoy.

Serves 4

Nutrition Information Per serving (approx. 2 cups):
Calories: 229
% of calories from fat: 7%
Sugar: 8 gm
Calcium: 6%
Iron: 13%
Vitamin C: 24%
Vitamin A: 32%
