Personal Pizzas


Ingredients:
1 t. oregano
1 can ready to bake biscuits
1 green onion
4 oz. low-fat mozzarella cheese
1/2 t. garlic salt
1/3 small can of crushed pineapple
1/4 t. pepper
2 oz. tomato paste
1 bunch broccoli

Materials:
Cookie sheet
Knife
Cheese grater

Cutting board
Can opener

Directions:
1. Wash hands thoroughly.
2. Open biscuit can and place biscuits on oiled cookie sheet.
3. Flatten each biscuit with the palm of your hand.
4. Spread 1 teaspoon of tomato paste of top of each biscuit.
5. Sprinkle paste with spices.
6. Grate cheese.
7. Place half of the grated cheese to the top of the paste.
8. Wash vegetables and dry with paper towel.
9. Dice green onion and chop broccoli into small pieces.
10. Place cut vegetables and pineapple on top of cheese.
11. Add remainder of cheese to the top of pizzas.
12. Bake for 10-15 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Watch to be sure that they do not burn.

Nutrition Information per Serving 5 (2 pizzas):
Calories: 192
% of calories from fat: 23%
Sugar: 4 gm
Calcium: 21%
Iron: 12%
Vitamin C: 98%
Vitamin A: 12%
Servings of fruits and vegetables: 1.25
