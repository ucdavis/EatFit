Fast Mix



Ingredients:
3 cups of any iron fortified cereal (Cheerios, Wheaties, Chex, Kix...)
1 cup dried fruit (raisins, banana, cranberries, mango, pineapple, apricot...)
1/2 cup of nuts (peanuts, sunflower seeds, cashews...)

Materials:
Medium sized bowl
1 cup dry measuring cup
4 plastic baggies
Spoon

Directions:
1. Wash hands thoroughly.
2. Put cereal, dried fruit and nuts in a medium sized bowl.
3. Gently mix ingredients.
4. Put mixture in plastic bags.
5. Enjoy with a glass of 100% juice or low-fat milk!

Makes 4 servings

Nutrition Information Per Serving (1 1/8 cup):
Calories: 389
% of calories from fat: 24%
Sugar: 27g
Calcium: 4%
Iron: 92%
Vitamin C: 18%
Vitamin A: 13%
Servings of fruits and vegetables: 1
